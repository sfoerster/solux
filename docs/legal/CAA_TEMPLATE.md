## 1. Definitions

1. "Project" means the software project known as `solus` and related documentation.
2. "Contribution" means any code, documentation, or other material intentionally submitted by Contributor for inclusion in the Project.
3. "Assigned Rights" means all right, title, and interest in and to the Contribution, including copyrights and related rights.

## 2. Assignment

Contributor irrevocably assigns to Project Owner all Assigned Rights in each Contribution submitted on or after the Effective Date.

## 3. Further Assurances

Contributor agrees to execute additional documents reasonably necessary to perfect, record, or enforce this assignment.

## 4. Fallback License

If any Assigned Right cannot be assigned under applicable law, Contributor grants Project Owner a perpetual, worldwide, exclusive, irrevocable, royalty-free license (with right to sublicense) to exercise that right to the fullest extent permitted by law.

## 5. Patent Rights

Contributor assigns to Project Owner all patent rights in the Contribution and, to the extent assignment is unavailable, grants an exclusive, irrevocable, royalty-free patent license for claims necessarily infringed by the Contribution.

## 6. Representations

Contributor represents that:

1. Contributor has sufficient rights to enter this Agreement and make the assignment.
2. Contributor's submissions do not knowingly infringe third-party rights.
3. Contributor has disclosed any third-party material included in a Contribution.

## 7. No Obligation to Accept

Project Owner has no obligation to accept or use any Contribution.

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of `________________________`, without regard to conflict-of-laws principles.

## 9. Entire Agreement

This Agreement is the entire agreement regarding assignment of rights in Contributions to the Project.
